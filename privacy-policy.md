# Privacy Policy for AU GradePilot

**Effective Date** July 2026

## Overview
AU GradePilot ("the Extension") respects your privacy. It is designed to run entirely locally within your web browser. 

## Data Collection and Usage
- **No Data Collection** The Extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or academic data on external servers.
- **Local Storage** All calculations, simulations, and settings are saved using Chrome's local storage mechanism (`chrome.storage.local`), which remains strictly on your device.
- **No Analytics** There is no telemetry, tracking, or analytics software embedded within the Extension.

## Third-Party Services
The Extension operates independently and does not use any third-party APIs or remote resources that could access your data.

## Changes
We may update this policy if new local-only features are added, but the core principle of non-transmission will remain.
