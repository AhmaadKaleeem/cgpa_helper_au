# GradePilot Extension Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

## Introduction
GradePilot respects your privacy. We built this tool to help you understand your academic standing without compromising your personal information. This document explains exactly what data the extension touches and how it operates.

## Information We Access
When you open your Air University portal, the extension reads the text currently displayed on your screen. This includes your course names, grades, and credit hours. It reads this data so the math engine can calculate your GPA. 

## Information We Do Not Collect
We never collect, store, or transmit your academic records. We do not know your name. We do not know your student ID. We do not know your grades. 

## Local Storage
Any settings you change or grades you simulate are saved strictly on your own computer. The extension uses the local storage provided by Google Chrome. This data is physically locked to your specific device and is never synced to the cloud. 

## Third Party Services
We use Google Analytics to monitor the technical performance of the extension. This helps us find bugs and understand which features are popular. 

We only track basic interactions. For example we track when the extension opens or when a button is clicked. We never transmit your academic data to Google. All analytics are tied to a randomly generated anonymous ID. 

## Data Security
Because all processing happens locally inside your browser, there is no central database for hackers to attack. Your data is as secure as the computer you are currently using. 

## Changes
We may update this policy if we release new features. We will notify you of any major changes. The core promise will always remain the same. Your academic data stays with you.
