# AU GradePilot - User Manual

This manual explains how to install and use the AU GradePilot extension.

## 1. Installation

1. **Extract** If you received a ZIP file, extract it to a local folder. Otherwise, clone the repository from GitHub.
2. **Extensions Page** Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome//extensions/`.
3. **Developer Mode** Enable "Developer mode" using the toggle in the top right corner.
4. **Load Extension** Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder.
5. **Launch** Log into the Air University Student Portal and open your Grade Report. The extension will load automatically.

## 2. Navigation

When you navigate to your Grade Report, the extension scans your transcript data.

- **GPA Button** A floating button displaying your current CGPA will appear. You can drag and drop this button anywhere on the screen.
- **Open Panel** Click the button or press `Ctrl + Shift + G` to open the main dashboard.

### Overview Tab
Provides a summary of your academic data.
*   **Stats** View your CGPA, latest SGPA, total earned credits, and quality points.
*   **Progress** Displays graduation progress based on the total required credits for your degree.
*   **Exclusions Notice** A banner will appear if non-credit courses or Pass/Fail grades (e.g., Pre-Calculus, S/U) were found and excluded from the calculation.

### Plan Tab
Helps you determine the grades needed for a target CGPA.
*   **Target CGPA** Enter your desired CGPA.
*   **Reality Check** Displays the required average SGPA for remaining semesters and indicates the difficulty level.
*   **Roadmap** Provides a breakdown of the required SGPA per semester.
*   **Strategic Opportunities** Lists specific past courses eligible for retake (grades below B minus) according to Air University policy. Select a target grade from the dropdown to instantly view the estimated CGPA gain and click Simulate to apply it.
*   **Advanced Planning** Expand this section to manually input future courses and credits.

### Simulate Tab
Allows you to test hypothetical scenarios.
*   Use the search bar to locate a past course.
*   Check the box to simulate a retake.
*   Select a new grade from the dropdown to see its effect on your overall CGPA.

## 3. Settings

Click the gear icon to access configuration options.
*   **Program Level** Select Undergraduate or Graduate. This automatically adjusts graduation requirements and grading scales according to Air University policy.
*   **Total Degree Credits** Modify this value if your degree requires a different number of total credits.
*   **Remaining Semesters** Update the number of semesters you have left to ensure accurate roadmap pacing.
*   **Data Management** Clear cached data and simulations if necessary.
