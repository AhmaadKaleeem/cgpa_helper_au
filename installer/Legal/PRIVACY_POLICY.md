PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how GradePilot ("we", "our", or "the Software") handles data. GradePilot is built with a strict privacy-first architecture designed to keep your academic data secure and local to your machine.

2. Data Collection and Storage
GradePilot does not collect, transmit, or store your personal academic data on any remote servers. All academic information, grades, CGPA, and course details are processed and stored locally within your browser's secure local storage. Your grades never leave your computer.

3. Extension Permissions
The GradePilot browser extension requests access only to specific Air University student portal domains. This access is strictly used to read your current academic standing for the sole purpose of calculating your GPA within your local browser environment. We do not access, track, or read data on any other websites you visit.

4. Analytics
To help us improve the Software, GradePilot collects strictly anonymous, aggregated usage statistics via Google Analytics 4 (GA4). This data includes non-identifying information such as feature usage frequency, generic interaction events, and basic session duration. No personally identifiable information (PII), academic records, or student IDs are ever included in this analytics data.

5. Third-Party Services
The Software integrates with Google Analytics for the anonymous tracking described above. We do not sell, rent, or share any of your data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or external entities. 

6. Data Security
Because your academic data remains entirely local to your device, its security relies on the physical and digital security of your own computer and browser profile. We strongly recommend using secure passwords and locking your device when not in use.

7. Children's Privacy
GradePilot is intended for university students and does not knowingly target or collect any data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Software implies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us
For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the developer via the official repository or support channels.
